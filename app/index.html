Counting Down

This is a web app that can help you count down to different events in your life. When the pizza should arrive, how long it is until your birthday or when your child is due.

Input a date and time and start a count down. Share the link with friends or foes.

Development

Counting Down is built with AngularJS and MomentJS, with help of AngularStrap and AngularMomentJS
